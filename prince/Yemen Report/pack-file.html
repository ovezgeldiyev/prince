DID YOU KNOW?

Our Gift of Water projects provides a consistent source of clean and safe drinking water which lowers the risk of waterborne diseases and improving overall health. With access to clean water, people can practice proper hygiene such as handwashing and maintaining better sanitation conditions, resulting in a reduction in disease transmission.

Wells and Handpumps allow for irrigation, allowing communities to grow crops and sustain agriculture even in areas with limited rainfall. This beneﬁts both food security and economic stability. A reliable water sources from wells and pumps support livestock needs and make animal husbandry/breeding easier, which is critical in many rural areas.

COUNTRY PROFILE

In order to live, 21.7 million of my fellow Yemenis must rely on humanitarian aid as the country approaches its ninth year of growing strife. The war has left a path of destruction in its wake: the economy is in ruins, and families are dealing with increased violence, starvation, and illness. The severe water and sanitation crisis, which continues to claim countless lives, is one of Yemen's most pressing problems.

HOW YOU HAVE HELPED

Your donation will make it possible for the beneﬁciaries to get clean water from underground source. This is especially crucial in areas without centralised water supplies or with inadequate water infrastructure. Water wells will make it possible for people to obtain water for their daily needs, such as drinking, cooking, sanitation, and other necessities, lowering the risk of waterborne illnesses. The beneﬁciaries will also beneﬁt from a time-saving solution because they previously had to travel far distances to access water that was only moderately clean before this well was installed.
