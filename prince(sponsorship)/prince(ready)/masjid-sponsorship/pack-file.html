“When a man dies, his deeds come to an end except for three things: Sadaqah Jariyah (ongoing charity), knowledge which beneﬁts, or a child who prays for him.”

At the core of every Muslim community is a Masjid. The Masjid is a sanctuary for worshippers to pray and ponder as well as being used for amenities in the community. A fundamental role of the Masjid is to call to Islam in order to educate and spiritually enlighten people. Sadly, many communities in the Ummah are without a Masjid. Many communities have seen their Masjid destroyed in conﬂict or natural disasters while many areas have not even had the resources to  build one.
