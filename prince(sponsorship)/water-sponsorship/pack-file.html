THE BIGGER PICTURE

One Ummah have completed hundreds of water projects, inculding hand pumps, water wells and desalination plants.

Households no longer need to walk for miles in order to access clean and safe water.

One Ummah have built (and continue to build) water wells close to each Masjid, in order to provide clean water for drinking and wudu.

Thank You Message

We at One Ummah want to extend an extra thanks to you for supporting the building of a well.

Having now seen the progress on the well you're funding, we'd like to convey heartfelt appreciation from the communities beneﬁtting from your generosity.

Alhamdulillah, with your contributions, these communities now have access to clean, safe water-an essential they previously lacked.

Access to clean water, something many of us take for granted, has transformed their daily lives, enabling them to fulﬁl their basic needs which were once out of reach.

Today, Alhamdutillah, we're deeply grateful to have united as One Ummah and turned their aspirations into reality!
