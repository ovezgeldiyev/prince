FAMILY SPONSORSHIP

Thank You Message

We at One Ummah want to extend an extra thanks to you for supporting a needy family through our Family Sponsorship Programme.

Having now seen your donations put to good use, we’d like to convey heartfelt appreciation from the family beneﬁtting from your generosity.

Alhamdulillah, with your contributions, the family has received relief and you have helped unburden them from desperation and diﬃculty.

Access to basic necessities like food, water and clothing, things many of us take for granted, has transformed their daily lives, enabling them to fulﬁl their basic needs which were once out of reach.

Today, Alhamdulillah, we’re deeply grateful to have united as One Ummah and turned their aspirations into reality!
JazakAllah Khair

Allah (swt) says in the Qur’an
“Whoever saves one life, it is as if he has saved the whole of mankind.”
[5:32]

Millions of our brothers and sisters around the world are facing unprecedented levels of poverty. There are Syrian, Afghan, Pakistani and African families who are desperately in need of sponsorship. Sadly, they do not have the basic necessities to survive.

In Gaza, around 2 million people live in devastating and terrible conditions ravaged by war. In Afghanistan, over 10 million need humanitarian aid. In Syria, millions are lacking the basics to survive.

Mothers and fathers do not know how they will aﬀord basic life necessities for their children such as food, water, shelter, and education. Widows are struggling to feed and clothe their orphan children.

The Prophet Muhammad (SAW) said, ‘Whoever helps ease a diﬃculty in the world, Allah will grant him ease from a diﬃculty in this world and in the Hereafter’
[Sahih Muslim 2699]

We support families from Afghanistan, Lebanon, and Kenya, as well as displaced Syrian families from the earthquake disaster, who need help, plus those who are living in make-shift camps at the borders.

Over 685 million people worldwide don’t have food, water, or shelter.
