Thank You Message

As a sponsor of an orphan, One Ummah would like to extend a special thanks to you.

In appreciation of your generous donations, we are expressing gratitude on behalf of the children you are sponsoring.

Alhamdulillah, as a result of your donations, these children are now able to af ford the simplest of necessities, which many have previously been unable to afford.

Your generosity has opened up the possibility for them to go to school, have a warm meal, and access essential healthcare services. Your support has made a real difference in their lives, and we are grateful for your contribution.

Today, Alhamdulillah, we can only thank Allah that we have come together as One Ummah and made their dreams come true!

Jazakallahu Khairan.
